LEADERSHIP

Forty years of teaching, still students of the art.

The Stolsmarks have spent four decades studying directly under the Okinawan masters and bringing those teachings home to Waukesha.

PRESIDENT · OKDR INTERNATIONAL

Hanshi Neil Stolsmark

Hanshi · Kyoshi · Founder, Authentic Ancient Arts

Founder and director of Authentic Ancient Arts and the worldwide head of Okinawa Kobudo Doushi Rensei-Kai. One of the recognized first-generation American students of the late Hanshi Shugoro Nakazato (10th Dan, named a Cultural Intangible Asset by the Japanese government), and now a student of Hanshi Minoru Nakazato.

Appointed successor to Sensei Yoshiaki Gakiya, head of the Matayoshi kobudo lineage. Has taught, demonstrated, and competed across the United States, Canada, Europe, Australia, Japan, South Africa, and Okinawa — and has personally promoted over 175 black belts.

SENIOR INSTRUCTOR

Rocky Stolsmark

7th Dan · Shorin-Ryu Shorinkan Karate

Senior instructor at the honbu dojo and one of the most highly-ranked women in Okinawan karate worldwide — among the first women in the United States to achieve 7th Dan in Shorin-Ryu Okinawan karate-do.

Like Hanshi Stolsmark, she continues to train directly under the Okinawan masters and brings deep technical expertise to the dojo's juniors, adults, and women's programs.
