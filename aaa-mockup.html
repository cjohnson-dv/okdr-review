LINEAGE

Hanshi Neil Stolsmark

Founder of Authentic Ancient Arts and a senior member of Okinawa Kobudo Doushi Rensei-Kai. Direct lineage from Okinawa, four decades of teaching, and a continuing student himself.

Our dojo carries forward the Shorin-Ryu Shorinkan tradition — not as a museum piece, but as a living practice that meets each student where they are.
